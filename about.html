About Us

Here at CinnaWoman, our original passion was centered on a single food: the cinnamon bun. We took a classic recipe and wondered how it might be made
better, how it might have better texture, greater balance, improved ingredients, and improved taste. And thus, through our hard labor and imagination, the cinnamon
roll was improved.

Due to this expansion of our vision, the expansion of our menu was a direct consequence. Hence, we now started trying out new and different combinations of
ingredients in order to still be roots-based while being innovative at the same time. In this manner, the present dessert menu of CinnaWoman now consists of
a rotation menu of over 16 different desserts, each one of which now takes as long as our regular buns.

What connects all of these desserts is that they each have a specific intention. Our dishes are designed, developed, and worked on until they reach
a level that satisfies us. This involves a balance between aspects like sweet and rich, soft and textured, traditional and innovative. It is a scenario
where we aim at presenting a dessert service that provides meaning through every single dessert that people order from us.
