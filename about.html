About Us

CinnaWoman began with one vision alone, and that was to somehow improve the cinnamon bun.
Today, our menu changes throughout the seasons and varies with the selection of quality raw materials we have available.
This allows us to produce a selection of quality desserts that make the most of honest and quality raw materials and our passion for what we do.
Each is made with our hands because we believe that quality desserts are made by honest people.
