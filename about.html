About Us

At CinnaWoman, our initial passion was for one thing: the cinnamon bun. We began by taking a traditional
recipe and wondering how it could be improved – how it could be better with texture, better balanced, better
ingredients, and better-tasting. And so, through hard work and imagination, we upgraded the cinnamon bun.

With the expansion of our vision, the expansion of our menu consequently took place as well.
Thus, we started experimenting with new and unique flavor combinations and ingredients in order to
remain roots-oriented while innovating at the same time. This way, the current dessert offerings of CinnaWoman
involve a rotational menu of more than 16 dessert options, each one of which currently takes the same amount of time and attention as our regular buns.
