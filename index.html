Welcome to CinnaWoman

At Cinnawoman, we believe that desserts are something you feel, not just something you consume. What we originally set out with, which is an appreciation
for traditional cinnamon buns, is now a place where tradition and innovation meet. Our cinnamon buns may be the focal point in what we do, but they certainly aren't
your ordinary cinnamon buns, not by any stretch of the imagination. Our cinnamon buns are designed, upgraded, and transformed to uncover more levels within.

However, Cinnawoman has slowly branched out from just offering dessert dishes originally on the menu. These days, we currently offer over 16 artistic dessert
dishes that feature changing flavors every now and then based on the season. This helps us get the most out of fresh ingredients we have access to, while also being
able to satisfy our customers' cravings for seasonal dishes, although these are less heavy when it is autumn/winter, but lighter when it becomes warmer.
