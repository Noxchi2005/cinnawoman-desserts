Welcome to CinnaWoman

At CinnaWoman, we believe that dessert is something you feel, as well as something you eat.
What we initially set out with as an appreciation for traditional cinnamon buns has evolved into an environment
where tradition and innovation come together.Our cinnamon buns may be central to what we do, but they aren’t your average
cinnamon buns by any means. Our cinnamon buns are designed, enhanced, and transformed to reveal further depths of flavor and texture.
However, CinnaWoman gradually diversified from its original product offering. Currently, our menu comprises over 16 artful dessert options
that change from time to time, according to the seasons. This enables us to make the most of the freshest produce available to us, as well as to provide
our customers with the flavors that are in season—not heavy and rich when it is colder, but lighter when the weather turns. There is always
something familiar and something new when you visit.
